Wildlife Locations in Sri Lanka

Horton Plains National Park

Location: Central Highlands

Horton Plains National Park is a scenic and biodiverse destination located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, the park features rolling hills, misty grasslands, and unique flora and fauna. One of its notable attractions is World's End, a sheer cliff with a stunning view of the surrounding plains. The park is also home to diverse wildlife, including sambar deer and a variety of bird species. Perfect for nature enthusiasts and hikers, Horton Plains National Park offers a captivating experience in the heart of Sri Lanka
